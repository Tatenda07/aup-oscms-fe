Help

1. How to Create an Account

For Students:

Click the "sign-up" toggle and select your college, enter your first and last name, school ID number, email, and phone number. Create a password and click "Submit". Click the "Log-in" toggle and sign in with your school ID number and password.


To View FAQs

Select FAQs from the menu to see if a question or concern you have has previously been addressed.


2. To Submit a Concern

Click the menu button in the top left corner, then click "Submit Concern"


3. To View Your Concern Status

Select "My Concerns" from the menu.


4. For SSO, CSC, and Admin:

Click the link at the bottom of the sign-up window that says "for administration. Log in with the correct email and password for your position and click "Submit"
