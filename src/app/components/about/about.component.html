About

The Online Student Concern Management System is a platform for the students of the Adventist University of the Philippines to voice their concerns directly to the school's staff who are in charge of handling and helping students. Before submitting a complaint consider checking our FAQ page, as your concern may have been submitted and resolved already. This is a safe space for students, so feel free to write your concerns while remaining respectful. Trust in the process and give time to those in charge to carefully assess and address your concern. We hope you have a good experience using our website.
